México Atemporal

México Atemporal is a contemporary music duo that explores the symbiosis between indigenous instruments of Mexico and Mesoamerica, the piano, and the electronic manipulation of sound in real time. This exploration is guided and based in the study of Mesoamerican codex, the Mexica dance tradition, and the study of classical and jazz pianist and composers such as Cage, Debussy, Armengol and Bill Evans. This unique collaboration between Christopher Garcia and Pablo Leñero seeks to intertwine the resonances of the past and present of Mexico.
